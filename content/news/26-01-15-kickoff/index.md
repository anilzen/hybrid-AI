The MURI project officially kicked off with an inaugural meeting held on January 15, 2026. The meeting brought together all principal investigators, co-investigators, and key collaborators to discuss the project's objectives, timelines, and collaborative strategies. Attendees engaged in productive discussions about the research goals and outlined the initial steps for the project's successful execution.
